Cinnamon Bun Recipe

Learn how to make Cinnamon Buns!

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour: 2 and 3/4 cup
  • Granulated Sugar: 1/4 cup
  • Brown Sugar: 1/3 cup
  • Salt: 1/4 cup
  • Whole: milk 3/4 cup
  • Butter: 3 TBSP, 3 TBSP, 2 TBSP
  • Yeast: 2 and 1/4 TSP
  • Egg: 1 large egg
  • Ground Cinnamon: 1 TBSP
  • Cream Cheese: 4 ounces
  • Pure Vanilla Extract: 1 TSP
  • Confectioners' Sugar: 2/3 cup
    •

    Instructions

  • Preheat your oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.
  • Mix your dry Ingredients in a big bowl (Flour, sugar, salt).
  • After that, warm the milk and butter together, and whisk in the yeast until it has dissolved.
  • Pour the mixture into the dry ingredient bowl, add an egg and mix together.
  • Once its formed into a dough place the dough on your work surface, and knead the dough.
  • How to knead dough
  • let the dough rest and settle for 10 minutes as you prepare the filling.
  • Roll out the dough the top it with softened butter and the brown sugar & cinnamon mixture.
  • Roll up the dough and use dental floss to evenly cut into 10-12 rolls.
  • Place rolls onto a lightly greased 9-10 inch pan.
  • Place your rolls in the oven for 24-27 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  • In a medium bowl using a handheld mixer, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy, then and the butter and beat until smooth.
  • After add in the sugar and vanillla until combined.
  • Once its in a smooth and fully mixed, spread the frosing over the buns with a knife or icing spatula.
